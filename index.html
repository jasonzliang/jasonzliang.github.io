Jason Zhi Liang, Ph.D.

AI Research Scientist & Principal Architect · Cognizant AI Labs · San Francisco, CA

Building agentic systems for System-2 reasoning. Scaling neuroevolution toward AGI.

I work toward Artificial General Intelligence through nature-inspired computation — at the intersection of evolutionary optimization, complexity science, and inference-time compute. I architected Caesar, an autonomous deep-research agent that scales test-time compute through adversarial self-refinement; earlier I invented CoDeepNEAT, the evolutionary neural-architecture-search algorithm now cited 1,500+ times. The throughline of a decade of work: population-based search and emergent structure as a path past gradient-only AI.
